BattleClaws
Itch.io | Socials

BattleClaws is a competitive, suspenseful PVP game that pits 2-4 players against each other in short bursts of timed gameplay. Each round, the lowest scoring player is eliminated until there is a victor.

Role: Programmer, 3D artist

Latest News: BattleClaws is going to GamerFest! See more at our Socials

Contributions

For the development of BattleClaws I have acted as a Lead Programmer, developing the player control, multiplayer, data, and round handling components.
I also created the model for the claws and other models such as the arena in both the demo and rebuild edition. BattleClaws has grown well as a project and
has been featured at Dublin Comic Con, and soon Gamerfest.
Folamh
Itch.io | Gameplay Video

Folamh is a game created for the Autumn 2023 GN! Games Jam. Folamh explores dark themes in a world of self hatred and destruction.

Role: Programmer, 3D artist

Latest News: Folamh is going to Kummi Gallery Helsinki this May!

Contributions

For Folamh I developed the character creation system, player model and designs, and some additional tech art for the games shaders.
Folamh will be featured in the Kummi Art Gallery as part of the Aalto GN! exhibition this May.
